In an age of wireless noise, the Gemini stands silent. Our proprietary shielding suppresses electromagnetic interference from modern signals, providing a noise floor that is effectively subterranean.

The Gyro-Stabilized Core

The "Gyro" in our name refers to our floating transformer mount. By suspending the power supply on a triple-axis silicone cradle, we eliminate 99.9% of mechanical vibration before it reaches the output stage.
